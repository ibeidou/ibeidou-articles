He marries Li Qiao, whoisn’t willing to sleep with him because of the university exam that she’s very eager to prepare.He waits so long for her that he can hardly bear it.Not able to endure the humiliation any longer, he sends his wife to her mother’s home. Time passes, and Wang Liang hears that his wife is going to the city in search of jobs and wants a divorce as well. Wang begs her to come home to him.In order to survive, Li Qiao sells her own body. However, it is nothing to be compared withher drastic change of heart. Wang is gradually forced to the edge of insanity…As the city changes, so changes everyone in it.
